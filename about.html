Our Story

Zinn's Wellness was born from a simple yet profound belief: that nature holds the key to true healing and well-being. Founded in 2024 by wellness expert Precious Okenwa, our spa has become a beloved sanctuary for those seeking authentic, natural approaches to health and relaxation.

After years of studying holistic healing practices around the world, Precious returned home with a vision to create a space where ancient wisdom meets modern comfort. Today, Zinn's Wellness stands as a testament to that vision—a place where every treatment, every product, and every moment is designed to honor the healing power of nature.

All About Your Spa Day

1. What should I wear to the spa?
We recommend wearing comfortable clothing to the spa. For treatments like massages or facials, you'll be provided with a robe and slippers. Our therapists are trained to drape you discreetly to ensure your privacy at all times.
2. Do I need to book an appointment in advance?
Yes, we highly recommend booking in advance to ensure availability for your preferred time and treatment.
3. What time should I arrive for my appointment?
Please arrive at least 15 minutes early to allow time for check-in and relaxation before your treatment begins.
4. Can I request a male or female therapist?
Yes, we accommodate requests for male or female therapists whenever possible. Please make this request when booking.
